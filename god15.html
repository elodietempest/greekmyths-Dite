EPIMETHEUS

Epimetheus is a Titan in Greek mythology, brother of Prometheus. His name means “afterthought.” He is known for accepting Pandora as a wife, leading to the release of evils into the world.

  • Roles & Attributes: Titan representing afterthought and human error.
  • Family & Origin: Son of Iapetus; brother of Prometheus, Atlas, Menoetius.
  • Myth: Married Pandora; known for opening Pandora’s box unintentionally.
