MEDUSA

Medusa was a mortal Gorgon with snakes for hair whose gaze could turn people to stone. Once a beautiful maiden, she was cursed by Athena and ultimately slain by Perseus.

  • Roles & Attributes: Mortal Gorgon, petrifying gaze, cursed by Athena.
  • Family & Origin: Daughter of Phorcys and Ceto.
  • Myth: Slain by Perseus; Pegasus and Chrysaor sprang from her blood.
