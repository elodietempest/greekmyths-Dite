CRIUS

Crius is a Titan in Greek mythology, son of Uranus and Gaia. He is one of the twelve Titans and father of Astraeus, Pallas, and Perses.

  • Roles & Attributes: Titan of constellations and father of deities associated with stars and warfare.
  • Family & Origin: Son of Uranus and Gaia; father of Astraeus, Pallas, and Perses.
  • Myth: Participated in the Titanomachy, the battle against the Olympians.
