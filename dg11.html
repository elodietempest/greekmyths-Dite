IDOMENEUS

Idomeneus is a Greek hero and demigod, king of Crete, who fought in the Trojan War. He was a respected warrior and leader among the Greek forces.

  • Roles & Attributes: Demigod hero, king of Crete, warrior in the Trojan War.
  • Family & Origin: Son of Deucalion; king of Crete.
  • Myth: Famous for leadership in the Trojan War and survival after the fall of Troy.
