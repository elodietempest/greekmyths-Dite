TALOS

Talos is a giant bronze automaton who guarded Crete by throwing stones at invaders. Created by Hephaestus, he had a single vein sealed with a bronze nail; removing it caused his death.

  • Roles & Attributes: Bronze giant, automaton, guardian of Crete.
  • Family & Origin: Created by Hephaestus, god of craftsmanship.
  • Myth: Represents protection, mechanical power, and ancient technology.
