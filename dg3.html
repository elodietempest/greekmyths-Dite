DIOMEDES

Diomedes is a Greek hero and king of Argos, son of Tydeus and Deipyle. He fought bravely in the Trojan War and was known for his skill in battle and strategic intelligence.

  • Roles & Attributes: Demigod hero, warrior, strategist of the Trojan War.
  • Family & Origin: Son of Tydeus and Deipyle, king of Argos.
  • Myth: Known for fighting alongside Odysseus and wounding Ares and Aphrodite in battle.
