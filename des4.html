EOS

Eos is the Titaness of the dawn in Greek mythology. She opens the gates of heaven for the Sun to rise and is known for her beauty and the gentle arrival of morning.

  • Roles & Attributes: Titaness of the dawn, bringer of morning light.
  • Family & Origin: Daughter of Hyperion and Theia; sister of Helios and Selene.
  • Myth: Rides across the sky in a chariot, announcing the sunrise.
