NYMPHS

Nymphs are enchanting minor goddesses of nature, dwelling in forests, rivers, mountains, and groves. They are beautiful, long-lived, and associated with fertility and natural spirits.

  • Roles & Attributes: Nature spirits, attendants of gods, protectors of natural places.
  • Family & Origin: Born of Gaia or other primordial deities.
  • Myth: Serve gods like Artemis and Dionysus; can bless or curse humans who disturb their domain.
