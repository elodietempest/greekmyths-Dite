GIGANTES

The Gigantes are colossal warriors born from Gaia’s rage. They waged war against the Olympian gods in the Gigantomachy but were defeated with the help of Heracles.

  • Roles & Attributes: Giant warriors, born of Gaia, immense strength.
  • Family & Origin: Children of Gaia alone.
  • Myth: Represent rebellion against divine authority and chaos versus order.
