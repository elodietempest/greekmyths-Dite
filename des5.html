CLYMENE

Clymene is a Titaness associated with fame and renown. She is often credited as the mother of Phaethon and the wife of Iapetus in Greek mythology.

  • Roles & Attributes: Titaness of fame, renown, and maternal figure.
  • Family & Origin: Daughter of Oceanus and Tethys; wife of Iapetus.
  • Myth: Mother of Phaethon, who attempted to drive the chariot of the sun.
