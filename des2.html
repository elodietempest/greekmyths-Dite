RHEA

Rhea is a Titaness and mother of the first generation of Olympian gods. She is associated with motherhood, fertility, and the protection of her children.

  • Roles & Attributes: Titaness of motherhood, fertility, and generation.
  • Family & Origin: Daughter of Uranus and Gaia; mother of Zeus, Hera, Hades, Poseidon, Demeter, and Hestia.
  • Myth: Saved Zeus from Cronus by hiding him at birth.
