CHARYBDIS

Charybdis is a monstrous sea entity who creates massive whirlpools capable of swallowing entire ships. Sailors navigating the narrow strait with Scylla faced great danger.

  • Roles & Attributes: Sea monster, whirlpools, destructive natural force.
  • Family & Origin: Offspring of Poseidon and Gaia in some myths.
  • Myth: Represents uncontrollable natural forces; Odysseus had to carefully navigate past her.
