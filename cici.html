CIRCE

Circe is a powerful enchantress and sorceress in Greek mythology who lived on the island of Aeaea. She was famous for her magical abilities and knowledge of potions and spells. Circe is best known for her encounter with the hero Odysseus, where she transformed his crew into animals before eventually helping him continue his journey home.

  • Roles & Attributes: Enchantress and sorceress known for powerful magic and potions. Her symbols include the magic wand, potion bowl, and animals.
  • Family & Origin: Daughter of the sun god Helios and the ocean nymph Perse.
  • Myth: Famous for turning Odysseus's men into pigs before later assisting Odysseus on his journey.
