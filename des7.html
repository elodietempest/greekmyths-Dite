TETHYS

Tethys is a Titaness of the sea and consort of Oceanus. She is the mother of river gods and Oceanids, representing the nourishing aspects of the ocean.

  • Roles & Attributes: Titaness of the sea, mother of rivers and ocean nymphs.
  • Family & Origin: Daughter of Uranus and Gaia; consort of Oceanus.
  • Myth: Provides waters for all rivers and lakes; mother of the Oceanids.
