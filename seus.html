PERSEUS

Perseus is a legendary hero and demigod in Greek mythology, son of Zeus and Danaë. He is best known for slaying the Gorgon Medusa and rescuing Andromeda from a sea monster. Perseus is celebrated for his courage, intelligence, and the use of divine gifts, including Hermes' winged sandals and Hades' helm of invisibility, which aided him in his heroic quests.

  • Roles & Attributes: Demigod hero, slayer of Medusa, rescuer of Andromeda. Known for bravery, cunning, and divine aid.
  • Family & Origin: Son of Zeus and Danaë.
  • Myth: Famous for defeating Medusa, saving Andromeda, and founding Mycenae.
