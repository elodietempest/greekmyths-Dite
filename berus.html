GREEK-MYTHOLOGY

SEBERUS

Cerberus, the monstrous, usually three-headed hound of Hades, famously guarded the Underworld gates, preventing the dead from leaving and the living from entering. Offspring of monsters Typhon and Echidna, he was best known for being captured by Heracles in his 12th labor and charmed by Orpheus’ music.
