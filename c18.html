EMPUSA

Empusa is a shape-shifting demon serving Hecate. She seduced young men before revealing her horrifying form and devouring them, with one bronze leg and one donkey leg in some myths.

  • Roles & Attributes: Shape-shifter, demon of nightmares and fear.
  • Family & Origin: Servant of Hecate, goddess of magic.
  • Myth: Represents deception, fear of the unknown, and nightmares.
