NYX

Nyx is the primordial goddess of the night. Born from Chaos, she is a powerful and shadowy figure whose presence is felt throughout the cosmos.

  • Roles & Attributes: Primordial goddess of night and darkness.
  • Family & Origin: Born from Chaos; mother of Aether, Hemera, Morpheus, and other deities.
  • Myth: Represents the night and mystery, commanding respect even from Zeus.
