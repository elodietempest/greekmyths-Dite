APHRODITE

Aphrodite is the Greek goddess of love, beauty, pleasure, and desire. She is one of the twelve Olympian gods and is known for her incredible beauty and charm. Aphrodite had the power to make gods and mortals fall in love and was often associated with romance and attraction. She was widely worshipped in ancient Greece as the goddess who influenced love and relationships.

  • Roles & Attributes: Goddess of love, beauty, passion, and fertility. Her symbols include the dove, rose, seashell, and mirror.
  • Family & Origin: According to myth, Aphrodite was born from sea foam near Cyprus after the Titan Uranus was defeated.
  • Children: Mother of Eros, Harmonia, and Aeneas among others.
