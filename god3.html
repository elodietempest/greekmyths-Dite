CHAOS

Chaos is the primordial void from which everything originated in Greek mythology. It is the first of all beings, representing the gaping nothingness before the creation of the cosmos.

  • Roles & Attributes: Primordial void, source of creation.
  • Family & Origin: The first being, giving rise to Gaia, Tartarus, Eros, Nyx, and Erebus.
  • Myth: The starting point of creation in Hesiod's Theogony.
