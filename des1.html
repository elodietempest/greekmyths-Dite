GAIA

Gaia is the primordial goddess of the Earth. She is the mother of all life and the foundation of the cosmos, giving birth to the sky, mountains, and sea.

  • Roles & Attributes: Primordial goddess of Earth, mother of Titans and gods.
  • Family & Origin: Born from Chaos; mother of Uranus, Ourea, Pontus, and the Titans.
  • Myth: Represents fertility, creation, and life on Earth.
