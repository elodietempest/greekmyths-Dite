TARTARUS

Tartarus is the primordial deity and abyss in Greek mythology, representing the deep dungeon of torment for the wicked and the prison of the Titans.

  • Roles & Attributes: Personification of the deep abyss and underworld prison.
  • Family & Origin: Born from Chaos alongside Gaia and Eros.
  • Myth: Holds the Titans and serves as the dark underworld pit for punishment.
