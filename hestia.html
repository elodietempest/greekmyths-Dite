HESTIA

Hestia is the Greek goddess of the hearth, home, and family. She is one of the twelve Olympian gods and is known for her peaceful and nurturing nature.

  • Roles & Attributes: Goddess of hearth, family, domesticity, and home.
  • Family & Origin: Daughter of Cronus and Rhea; sister of Zeus, Hera, Poseidon, Demeter, and Hades.
  • Myth: Protector of domestic harmony and sacred fire of the home and city.
