THEMIS

Themis is the Titaness of divine law, order, and justice. She represents fairness and the natural law that governs gods and humans.

  • Roles & Attributes: Titaness of law, justice, and order.
  • Family & Origin: Daughter of Uranus and Gaia; consort of Zeus.
  • Myth: Often depicted as an advisor to Zeus and the gods on justice and order.
