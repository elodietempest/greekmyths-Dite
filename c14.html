ECHIDNA

Echidna, the Mother of Monsters, is half-woman and half-serpent. She gave birth to terrifying creatures such as Cerberus, the Hydra, and the Chimera, remaining a shadowy figure in mythology.

  • Roles & Attributes: Mother of monsters, half-woman half-serpent.
  • Family & Origin: Child of Phorcys and Ceto.
  • Myth: Represents the primordial source of chaos and monstrous lineage.
