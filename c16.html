LAMIA

Lamia was once a queen loved by Zeus, but Hera’s jealousy cursed her. She became a child-devouring monster and haunted the night, preying on the young.

  • Roles & Attributes: Monster, child-devourer, cursed queen.
  • Family & Origin: Mortal queen transformed by divine curse.
  • Myth: Represents madness, jealousy, and tragic transformation.
