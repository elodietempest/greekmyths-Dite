BELLEROPHON

Bellerophon is a hero and demigod in Greek mythology, son of Glaucus and Eurymede (or by Poseidon in some accounts). He is most famous for taming the winged horse Pegasus and defeating the monstrous Chimera. Bellerophon represents courage, skill, and the archetype of a heroic warrior who achieves greatness with divine assistance.

  • Roles & Attributes: Demigod hero, tamer of Pegasus, and slayer of monsters like the Chimera.
  • Family & Origin: Son of Glaucus and Eurymede, or sometimes said to be a son of Poseidon.
  • Myth: Famous for defeating the Chimera and his tragic hubris that led him to attempt to reach Olympus.
