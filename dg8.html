AEOLUS

Aeolus is the ruler of the winds in Greek mythology, sometimes considered a demigod. He controlled the winds and helped heroes like Odysseus on their journeys.

  • Roles & Attributes: God or demigod of winds, controller of storms and calm seas.
  • Family & Origin: Son of Hippotes in some myths.
  • Myth: Gifted Odysseus the bag of winds to aid his voyage home.
