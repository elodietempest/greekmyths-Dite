ATHENA

Athena is the Greek goddess of wisdom, war strategy, and crafts. She is one of the twelve Olympian gods and is known for her intelligence, courage, and fairness. Athena was the protector of the city of Athens and was highly respected by both gods and mortals. Unlike Ares, who represented violent war, Athena symbolized strategic warfare and wisdom.

  • Roles & Attributes: Goddess of wisdom, strategic warfare, crafts, and justice. Her symbols include the owl, olive tree, shield, and spear.
  • Family & Origin: Daughter of Zeus. According to myth, Athena was born fully grown and armored from Zeus's head.
  • Influence: Protector of heroes such as Odysseus and Perseus, and patron goddess of the city of Athens.
