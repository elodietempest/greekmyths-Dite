AETHER

Aether is the primordial god of the upper air, light, and the bright sky. He is the son of Erebus and Nyx and represents the pure upper atmosphere that the gods breathe.

  • Roles & Attributes: God of upper air and light.
  • Family & Origin: Son of Erebus (Darkness) and Nyx (Night).
  • Myth: Represents the clear, bright upper sky, contrasting with Erebus.
