HARPIES

Harpies are winged spirits with female faces and bird bodies. They served as agents of punishment, snatching food and tormenting mortals like King Phineus until the Argonauts intervened.

  • Roles & Attributes: Winged spirits, agents of punishment.
  • Family & Origin: Born of Thaumas and Electra.
  • Myth: Represent divine retribution and the fury of the winds.
