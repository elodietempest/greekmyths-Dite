SPHINX

The Sphinx has the body of a lion, wings of a bird, and the head of a woman. She challenged travelers with riddles; those who failed were devoured. Oedipus famously solved her riddle.

  • Roles & Attributes: Guardian, riddler, hybrid creature.
  • Family & Origin: Child of Echidna and Orthus in some myths.
  • Myth: Defeated by Oedipus; symbolizes wisdom, mystery, and deadly consequence of ignorance.
