SATYRS

Satyrs are half-man, half-goat creatures who follow Dionysus. They revel in music, dance, and wild celebration, representing the untamed and instinctual side of nature.

  • Roles & Attributes: Mischievous nature spirits, followers of Dionysus.
  • Family & Origin: Offspring of Pan or other nature deities in myths.
  • Myth: Symbolize instinct, pleasure, and freedom from restraint.
