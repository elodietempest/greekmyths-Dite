THESEUS

Theseus is a legendary hero and demigod in Greek mythology, son of Aegeus (or Poseidon in some myths) and Aethra. He is famous for slaying the Minotaur in the Labyrinth of Crete and uniting Attica under Athens.

  • Roles & Attributes: Demigod hero, slayer of the Minotaur, founder-king of Athens.
  • Family & Origin: Son of King Aegeus of Athens and Aethra; sometimes said to be a son of Poseidon.
  • Myth: Famous for defeating the Minotaur, navigating the Labyrinth, and performing heroic feats across Greece.
