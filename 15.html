GRIFFIN

The Griffin has the body of a lion and the head and wings of an eagle. It is a guardian of treasures and symbolizes strength, vigilance, and divine protection.

  • Roles & Attributes: Hybrid creature, guardian, symbol of power.
  • Family & Origin: Mythical creature, often considered offspring of divine beasts.
  • Myth: Guarded gold and sacred places; symbolizes vigilance and strength.
