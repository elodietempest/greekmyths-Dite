MINOS

Minos is a legendary king of Crete and a demigod in Greek mythology, son of Zeus and Europa. He is famous for commissioning the Labyrinth to contain the Minotaur and for being a wise judge in the underworld after his death.

  • Roles & Attributes: King of Crete, demigod, founder of Cretan law and civilization.
  • Family & Origin: Son of Zeus and Europa.
  • Myth: Ordered the construction of the Labyrinth to imprison the Minotaur and later became a judge in Hades.
