PHOEBE

Phoebe is a Titaness of prophecy and intellect. She is the daughter of Uranus and Gaia and the grandmother of Apollo and Artemis.

  • Roles & Attributes: Titaness of intellect, prophecy, and the moon.
  • Family & Origin: Daughter of Uranus and Gaia; mother of Leto and grandmother of Apollo and Artemis.
  • Myth: Associated with prophetic powers and the guidance of divine knowledge.
