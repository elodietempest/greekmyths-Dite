ACHILLES

Achilles is the greatest Greek hero of the Trojan War, son of the sea goddess Thetis and King Peleus. He is known for his strength, speed, and near invincibility, except for his famous heel.

  • Roles & Attributes: Demigod hero, warrior of the Trojan War, known for unmatched combat skills.
  • Family & Origin: Son of Thetis and Peleus.
  • Myth: Central figure in the Iliad; killed Hector and was eventually killed by an arrow to his heel.
