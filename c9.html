SCYLLA

Scylla is a monstrous sea creature with multiple heads that devoured sailors passing her strait. Once a beautiful sea nymph, she was transformed into a monster by magic and jealousy.

  • Roles & Attributes: Multi-headed sea monster, devourer of sailors.
  • Family & Origin: Former nymph, transformed by Circe.
  • Myth: Odysseus chose to sail near her, losing six men; symbolizes unavoidable sacrifice and the consequences of jealousy.
