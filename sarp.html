SARPEDON

Sarpedon is a demigod in Greek mythology, the son of Zeus and Laodamia (or Europa in some myths). He was a hero of the Trojan War, fighting on the side of the Trojans. Renowned for his bravery, strength, and loyalty, Sarpedon was favored by the gods, and Zeus even considered saving him during battle, but ultimately allowed fate to take its course.

  • Roles & Attributes: Demigod warrior of Troy, known for courage, strength, and skill in battle.
  • Family & Origin: Son of Zeus and Laodamia (or Europa).
  • Myth: Fought and died in the Trojan War; honored by Zeus and the gods.
