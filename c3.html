MINOTAUR

The Minotaur had the body of a man and the head of a bull. Born from a curse on Queen Pasiphaë, it lived in the Labyrinth on Crete until slain by Theseus.

  • Roles & Attributes: Monster with bull’s head, imprisoned in the Labyrinth.
  • Family & Origin: Offspring of Pasiphaë and a sacred bull.
  • Myth: Killed by Theseus; symbolizes chaos and punishment for defiance of the gods.
