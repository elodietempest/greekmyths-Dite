PEGASUS

Pegasus is the majestic winged horse born from the blood of Medusa when she was slain. He became a companion of Bellerophon and later carried Zeus’s thunderbolts.

  • Roles & Attributes: Winged horse, symbol of inspiration and freedom.
  • Family & Origin: Born from Medusa and Poseidon’s influence.
  • Myth: Helped heroes like Bellerophon; later ascended to the heavens as a constellation.
