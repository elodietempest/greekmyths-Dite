ORION

Orion is a giant huntsman in Greek mythology who was placed among the stars as the constellation Orion. He is known for his strength, hunting skills, and tragic death.

  • Roles & Attributes: Demigod hunter, giant warrior, associated with the constellation Orion.
  • Family & Origin: Son of Poseidon and Euryale (or Hyrieus in some myths).
  • Myth: Famous for hunting adventures and being immortalized as a constellation.
