NELEUS

Neleus is a demigod hero in Greek mythology, son of Poseidon and Tyro. He became king of Pylos and is known for founding a lineage of heroes including Nestor.

  • Roles & Attributes: Demigod hero, king of Pylos.
  • Family & Origin: Son of Poseidon and Tyro; father of Nestor.
  • Myth: Famous for his rule in Pylos and his descendants’ participation in heroic myths.
