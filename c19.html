MANTICORE

The Manticore has the body of a lion, a human face, and a scorpion’s tail that fires venomous spines. It devours its victims entirely and symbolizes hidden danger.

  • Roles & Attributes: Lion body, human face, scorpion tail; deadly predator.
  • Family & Origin: Legendary hybrid creature.
  • Myth: Represents hidden danger and lethal deception.
