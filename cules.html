HERCULES

Hercules, known as Heracles in Greek mythology, is one of the greatest demigod heroes. He is the son of Zeus and Alcmene. Famous for his extraordinary strength, courage, and heroic deeds, Hercules is best known for completing the Twelve Labors, a series of seemingly impossible tasks assigned to him as penance. He is revered as a symbol of endurance and heroism.

  • Roles & Attributes: Demigod hero, known for immense strength, bravery, and completing the Twelve Labors.
  • Family & Origin: Son of Zeus and Alcmene.
  • Myth: Famous for the Twelve Labors, defeating monsters, and his adventures across Greece and beyond.
