MELEAGER

Meleager is a hero and demigod in Greek mythology, the son of King Oeneus of Calydon and Althaea. He is best known as the leader of the Calydonian Boar Hunt, a legendary adventure where many Greek heroes gathered to slay the monstrous boar sent by Artemis. Meleager is celebrated for his bravery, leadership, and tragic fate connected to the magical log that determined his lifespan.

  • Roles & Attributes: Demigod hero, leader of the Calydonian Boar Hunt, known for courage and skill in battle.
  • Family & Origin: Son of King Oeneus and Althaea.
  • Myth: Famous for the Calydonian Boar Hunt and the story of the magical log that determined his life.
