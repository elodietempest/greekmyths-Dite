ATLAS

Atlas is a Titan condemned to hold up the heavens for eternity after the Titanomachy. He is the son of Iapetus and Clymene.

  • Roles & Attributes: Titan of endurance, supporter of the heavens.
  • Family & Origin: Son of Iapetus and Clymene; brother of Prometheus, Epimetheus, Menoetius.
  • Myth: Punished by Zeus to hold the sky for eternity.
