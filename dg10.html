TEUCER

Teucer is a Greek hero and demigod, son of Telamon and Hesione. He was a skilled archer who fought in the Trojan War and is credited with founding settlements after the war.

  • Roles & Attributes: Demigod hero, archer, warrior of the Trojan War.
  • Family & Origin: Son of Telamon and Hesione; brother of Ajax.
  • Myth: Survived the Trojan War and established new cities in Cyprus and Salamis.
