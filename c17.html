CHIMERA

The Chimera is a monstrous fire-breathing hybrid: a lion’s body, a goat’s head on its back, and a serpent’s tail. Born of Typhon and Echidna, it was defeated by Bellerophon riding Pegasus.

  • Roles & Attributes: Fire-breathing hybrid, monstrous predator.
  • Family & Origin: Child of Typhon and Echidna.
  • Myth: Symbolizes chaos and destruction; defeated by Bellerophon.
