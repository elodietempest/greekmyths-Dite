HYDRA

The Lernaean Hydra is a multi-headed serpent; when one head is cut off, two more grow back. One head is immortal. Heracles defeated it with the help of Iolaus during his second labor.

  • Roles & Attributes: Multi-headed serpent, deadly poison, regenerative.
  • Family & Origin: Child of Typhon and Echidna.
  • Myth: Defeated by Heracles; symbolizes challenges that multiply if poorly handled.
