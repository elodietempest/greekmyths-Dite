SIRENS

Sirens are part-woman, part-bird creatures who lured sailors to their deaths with enchanting music and voices. Odysseus survived them by blocking his crew’s ears with wax.

  • Roles & Attributes: Lure sailors with song, dangerous temptation.
  • Family & Origin: Daughters of river god Achelous and Muse Calliope in some myths.
  • Myth: Tempted Odysseus; symbolize temptation and the dangers of desire.
