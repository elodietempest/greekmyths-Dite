HYPERION

Hyperion is a Titan god of light and the sun. He is the son of Uranus and Gaia, and father of Helios (Sun), Selene (Moon), and Eos (Dawn).

  • Roles & Attributes: Titan of light, father of celestial deities.
  • Family & Origin: Son of Uranus and Gaia; father of Helios, Selene, Eos.
  • Myth: Represents the sun, moon, and dawn in Greek mythology.
