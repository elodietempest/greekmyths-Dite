KRAKEN

The Kraken is a colossal sea monster with massive tentacles capable of capsizing ships. Its enormous size and terrifying appearance inspired sailors’ tales of the deep ocean.

  • Roles & Attributes: Colossal sea monster, terror of sailors.
  • Family & Origin: Legendary creature of the deep seas.
  • Myth: Threatened ships and sailors in Nordic and Greek legends; symbolizes the dangers of the unknown ocean.
