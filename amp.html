GREEK-MYTHOLOGY

AMPHITRITE

Amphitrite is the Greek goddess-queen of the sea, wife of Poseidon, and eldest of the Nereids (sea nymphs). As a major sea deity, she represents the calming power of the ocean, mothering sea creatures like dolphins and seals. She is frequently depicted alongside Poseidon in a chariot, often holding a trident.

Key Attributes

  • 👑 Goddess-Queen of the Sea
  • Wife of Poseidon.
  • She is identified with the Roman goddess Salacia, the goddess of saltwater.
  • 🏛️ Mount Olympus
