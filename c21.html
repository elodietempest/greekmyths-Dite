GERYON

Geryon is a giant with three bodies joined at the waist, who guarded his red cattle. He was killed by Heracles during the tenth labor.

  • Roles & Attributes: Three-bodied giant, cattle guardian.
  • Family & Origin: Child of Chrysaor and Callirrhoe in some myths.
  • Myth: Defeated by Heracles; represents monstrous strength and challenge.
