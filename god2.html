OCEANUS

Oceanus is a Titan god of the sea, the great river that encircled the world. He is the son of Uranus and Gaia, husband of Tethys, and father of the river gods and Oceanids.

  • Roles & Attributes: Titan of the ocean, father of river gods and sea nymphs.
  • Family & Origin: Son of Uranus and Gaia; consort of Tethys.
  • Myth: Represents the world-encircling ocean and all water bodies.
