IAPETUS

Iapetus is a Titan in Greek mythology, son of Uranus and Gaia. He is the father of Prometheus, Epimetheus, Atlas, and Menoetius.

  • Roles & Attributes: Titan of mortality and craftsmanship.
  • Family & Origin: Son of Uranus and Gaia; father of Prometheus, Epimetheus, Atlas, Menoetius.
  • Myth: Participated in the Titanomachy, representing human traits in his descendants.
