THEIA

Theia is a Titaness of sight and the shining light of the clear blue sky. She is the mother of Helios (Sun), Selene (Moon), and Eos (Dawn).

  • Roles & Attributes: Titaness of sight, shining light, and the sky.
  • Family & Origin: Daughter of Uranus and Gaia; mother of Helios, Selene, and Eos.
  • Myth: Represents the radiant light of the heavens.
