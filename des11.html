MNEMOSYNE

Mnemosyne is the Titaness of memory and remembrance. She is the mother of the nine Muses with Zeus and represents knowledge, memory, and oral tradition.

  • Roles & Attributes: Titaness of memory, language, and knowledge.
  • Family & Origin: Daughter of Uranus and Gaia; consort of Zeus.
  • Myth: Mother of the Muses, inspiring art, music, and storytelling.
