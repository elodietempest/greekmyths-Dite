EROS

Eros is the primordial god of love and sexual desire, later also considered the son of Aphrodite. He brings passion and desire among gods and mortals.

  • Roles & Attributes: God of love and desire. Symbols: bow, arrows, wings.
  • Family & Origin: Either primordial or son of Aphrodite.
  • Myth: Inspires love and attraction in gods and humans.
