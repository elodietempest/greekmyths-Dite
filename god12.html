COEUS

Coeus is a Titan in Greek mythology, associated with intellect and the axis of heaven around which the constellations revolve. He is the father of Leto and Asteria.

  • Roles & Attributes: Titan of intellect and the heavenly axis.
  • Family & Origin: Son of Uranus and Gaia; father of Leto and Asteria.
  • Myth: Participated in the Titanomachy, imprisoned by Zeus afterwards.
