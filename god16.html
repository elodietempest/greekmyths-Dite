ASTRAEUS

Astraeus is a Titan god of dusk and stars. He is the son of Crius and Eurybia and father of the four winds (Anemoi) and stars with his wife Eos (Dawn).

  • Roles & Attributes: Titan of dusk, stars, and astrology.
  • Family & Origin: Son of Crius and Eurybia; consort of Eos.
  • Myth: Father of the winds and stars; governs the night sky.
