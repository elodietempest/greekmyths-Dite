CYCLOPS

Cyclopes are one-eyed giants known for their immense strength and skill in metalworking. Some built the thunderbolts of Zeus and other weapons for the gods.

  • Roles & Attributes: One-eyed giant, master blacksmith, formidable strength.
  • Family & Origin: Children of Uranus and Gaia in most myths.
  • Myth: Assisted gods with forging weapons; Polyphemus is the famous Cyclops encountered by Odysseus.
