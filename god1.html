CRONUS

Cronus is the leader of the Titans in Greek mythology and the father of the first generation of Olympian gods. He overthrew his father Uranus and ruled during the mythological Golden Age before being overthrown by his son Zeus.

  • Roles & Attributes: Titan of time and harvest, ruler of the universe during the Golden Age.
  • Family & Origin: Son of Uranus and Gaia; father of Zeus, Hades, Poseidon, Hera, Demeter, and Hestia.
  • Myth: Overthrew Uranus, swallowed his children, then was overthrown by Zeus.
