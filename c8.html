CERBERUS

Cerberus is the three-headed dog who guards the gates of the Underworld, preventing the dead from escaping and the living from entering without permission. Heracles captured him as his final labor.

  • Roles & Attributes: Guardian of the Underworld, three-headed dog, loyal and terrifying.
  • Family & Origin: Child of Echidna and Typhon.
  • Myth: Captured by Heracles; symbolizes loyalty and boundary between life and death.
