ASTERIA

Asteria is the Titaness of stars and nocturnal oracles. She is the daughter of Coeus and Phoebe, and she is associated with prophetic powers and the night sky.

  • Roles & Attributes: Titaness of stars, nocturnal oracles, and astrology.
  • Family & Origin: Daughter of Coeus and Phoebe.
  • Myth: Mother of Hecate; transformed into a quail to escape pursuit by Zeus.
