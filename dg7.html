MNENON

Mnenenon is a Greek hero and demigod, son of Theseus and either Hippolyta or Antiope. Known for his chastity and devotion to Artemis, he became a tragic figure in Greek mythology due to the curse of Aphrodite.

  • Roles & Attributes: Demigod hero, devoted to Artemis, known for chastity and hunting skills.
  • Family & Origin: Son of Theseus and Hippolyta/Antiope.
  • Myth: Cursed by Aphrodite leading to his tragic death.
